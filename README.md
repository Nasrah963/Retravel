# Retravel
From time to time, people need to take a break from work or study to calm and relax their minds and prepare
them to return to work again actively, so they spend a lot of time searching for hotels and suitable trips for them,
and this search makes them distracted and waste time searching, and after searching for hotels and trips, they
also search for transportation to their destination. That is where the idea of a travel website came from. Re travel
is a website for all people who are looking for trips with accommodation and transportation, they have the ability to
book their destination and travel time and look at reviews about hotels and previous trips.
